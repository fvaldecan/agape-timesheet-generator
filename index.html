Works best on a laptop or desktop browser.

1. Set up the "Get My Schedule" bookmarklet/button (one time only)

Grabs your schedule automatically — no more copying it by hand.

Drag this to your bookmarks bar. Don't see the bar? Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Cmd+Shift+B on Mac).

🔗 Get My Schedule
Dragging isn't working? Copy and paste it in instead
  1. Right-click your bookmarks bar and choose Add page (or New bookmark).
  2. Name it Get My Schedule (or anything you'll recognize).
  3. For the web address / URL, copy everything in the box below and paste it in.
  4. Save.

To use it: open your Club Automation schedule (Tools → Staff Scheduling), click the button, then paste what it copies below.

2. Paste your schedule

Club Automation only shows one week at a time, so for a multi-week pay period, paste each week separately. Parse shows a breakdown first (blocked, empty, already added) — check it, then Add to sheet.

No bookmarklet?
  1. Right-click page and choose Inspect (or press Ctrl+Shift+I).
  2. In the Elements panel, find the <div id="court_schedule"> element.
  3. Right-click it and choose Copy → Copy outerHTML.

3. Your settings (remembered next time you come back)

Pay rates

Type a word from your booking titles (like "PL" or "Drill"), choose start or anywhere in the title, and set the pay. First match wins, so put specific rows above general ones. Per-person split is for group classes paid per attendee — you'll enter the headcount after pasting. "# people" is editable on every row (handy for a semi-private with 2 students).

⚠ You changed a rate. The sheet below is still showing the old numbers.
Compensation summary (optional)

Paste your contract's rate bullets here — same list you'd copy from a Word doc or PDF. Shows up at the bottom of your sheet, on screen and in the download.
